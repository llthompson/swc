# Sapphic Writers Circle Website Plan

## Site approach

Use the HTML5 UP Stellar template as a warm one-page homepage, but keep the structure scalable for future static pages.

## MVP goal

Create a static informational website that answers the questions someone might have before attending a meeting:

- What is Sapphic Writers Circle?
- Who is it for?
- Would I belong?
- Will I feel awkward?
- What happens at meetings?
- When and where do meetings happen?
- Why does the group exist?
- How do I join or get connected?

## MVP homepage sections

1. Hero
2. What is Sapphic Writers Circle?
3. What we do
4. What to expect at your first meeting
5. Who it is for
6. When and where we meet
7. FAQ
8. Join / Discord / Instagram

## Future content

Future pages or sections may include:

- Past event gallery
- Member bios
- Member work spotlights
- Past zines
- Event recaps
- Writing workshop information
- Local sapphic / queer writing resources

## Build rules

- Static HTML/CSS first
- No CMS
- No backend
- No database
- No framework
- Light JavaScript only if it improves the experience
- Keep copy warm, clear, welcoming, and low-pressure
- Prioritize mobile-first clarity
