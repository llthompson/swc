Sapphic Writers Circle

Community & creative support for sapphic writers in Austin, TX

About the group

Sapphic Writers Circle is a welcoming and non-judgemental writing group that meets in person every week in Austin, TX. We aim to create a safe, inclusive space for all those who identify as sapphic, regardless of their specific gender identity or sexual orientation.

We welcome writers at every stage of their craft. We have poets, novelists, essayists, screenwriters, and enjoy all forms of creative expression!

What to Expect

No one likes being the new person in a room, so we want you to feel welcome from the start. Every meeting starts with a short round of intros: name, pronouns, and what type of writing you enjoy. We are all here to build community, and every person who walks in the door helps us do that.

Beyond that, each meeting will look a little bit different! Here's a few of the activities we do:

  • Peer Workshops

    We give encouraging feedback on each other’s writing, with a focus on what's working and what we want to see more of!

  • Zines & Creative Projects

    We collaborate on themed zines and other projects, from brainstorming to reviewing submissions and putting everything together.

  • Odds & Ends

    Some weeks are more casual: writing exercises and games, free write time, or seasonal parties. These weeks may be super unstructured, or we may have them planned weeks in advance.
